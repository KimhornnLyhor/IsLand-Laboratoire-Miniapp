ISLAND LABORATOIRE EXPANDS ANTIDIABETIC PORTFOLIO WITH THREE NEW COMBINATION PRODUCTS

Island Laboratoire is proud to announce the addition of three new antidiabetic combination products to our growing portfolio — GLUCOEMPA-5/500, GLUCOEMPA-12.5/500, and the SITTINMET series. These new formulations combine SGLT2 inhibitors and DPP-4 inhibitors with metformin, reflecting the growing clinical preference for combination therapy in type 2 diabetes management.
