FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

How do I place a product order?
Contact our orders team via the form above or email orders@islandlaboratoire.com. Include the product name, INN, quantity and your organisation details. We will respond with pricing and availability within 2 business days.
Are your products available to individual patients?
No. Island Laboratoire is a B2B pharmaceutical manufacturer. Our products are supplied to licensed pharmacies, hospitals, clinics and authorised distributors only. Individual consumers should purchase through their pharmacy.
Can I request GMP certificates or product dossiers?
Yes. Contact our regulatory team at regulatory@islandlaboratoire.com with your organisation details and the specific documentation required. We support product registration in multiple markets.
What is your minimum order quantity?
Minimum order quantities vary by product. Contact our sales team with your requirements and we will provide tailored information based on your order volume and product range.
Do you offer distribution partnerships?
Yes, we work with regional distributors across multiple markets. If you are interested in a distribution agreement, please contact distribution@islandlaboratoire.com with details about your coverage area and existing distribution network.
Are all your products GMP-certified?
Yes. All Island Laboratoire products are manufactured under Good Manufacturing Practice guidelines. Our facility is regularly audited and maintains full GMP compliance. Documentation is available upon request.
