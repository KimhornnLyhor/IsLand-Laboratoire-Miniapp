Island Laboratoire was founded on a simple belief: that quality medicines should be accessible to every community that needs them. We manufacture, we supply, we care.

Our Mission

MEDICINES THAT MATTER

At Island Laboratoire, we exist to manufacture and supply pharmaceutical products of the highest quality — products that make a real difference in the lives of patients and the practitioners who care for them.

We operate with full transparency, using International Non-proprietary Names (INN) across our entire product range, ensuring that every formulation is traceable, reliable, and based on established science.

From a single tablet of paracetamol to a complex combination antidiabetic, every product that leaves our facility carries our commitment to quality, safety, and efficacy.

Our Journey

HOW WE GREW TOGETHER

FOUNDING

Island Laboratoire Established

Founded with a mission to manufacture quality pharmaceutical products for communities across the region. Initial portfolio focused on essential antibiotics and analgesics.

EXPANSION

Cardiovascular & Antidiabetic Range Launched

Responded to growing regional demand for chronic disease medicines by expanding into cardiovascular agents and antidiabetics — adding 25+ new products to the portfolio.

MILESTONE

GMP Certification Achieved

Achieved full Good Manufacturing Practice certification following comprehensive facility upgrades and quality system implementation — a landmark commitment to international standards.

TODAY

90+ Products Across 12 Categories

Island Laboratoire now manufactures over 90 products spanning 12 therapeutic categories, serving pharmacies, clinics and hospitals throughout the region as a trusted partner.
